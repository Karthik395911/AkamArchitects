## Light as a design material

We plan lighting early so it supports the architecture rather than interrupting it. Daylight, task light, accent light, exterior glow, fixture placement, control logic, and maintenance access are considered together.

## What the service includes

- Lighting concepts for interior and exterior spaces
- Fixture selection, placement, and beam direction
- Ambient, task, accent, and feature lighting layers
- Coordination with ceiling, electrical, and furniture layouts
- Site support for focusing and final adjustment

The result is lighting that feels quiet, intentional, and useful across different times of day.
