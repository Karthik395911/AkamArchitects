## Planning the build before the build begins

New construction gives us the opportunity to align site strategy, structure, services, materials, and construction sequencing from day one. We plan the building as a complete system, not a set of isolated decisions.

## What the service includes

- Site planning and building orientation
- Architectural design and construction documentation
- Consultant coordination for structure and services
- Construction-stage design support
- Material, budget, and site execution guidance

The result is a clearer path from concept to handover, with fewer compromises between design ambition and site reality.
